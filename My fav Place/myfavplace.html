Colorado Winter Park

Winter Park in Colorado is a magically place when you go in the Winter. It is always snowing and you can go skiing or snowboarding. But, last time we went my leg was in a cast so I couldn't go skiing. It was very sad. But it is still a miagical place, with hills and mountains and trees covered in snow! Colorado is the best place to go to in the Winter.
